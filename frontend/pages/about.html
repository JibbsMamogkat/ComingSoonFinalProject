About Pinoy Plates

Welcome to Pinoy Plates, your gateway to the rich and diverse flavors of the Philippines. We are dedicated to bringing you authentic Filipino dishes that capture the essence of traditional Filipino cuisine, made with love and care.

Our Mission

At Pinoy Plates, our mission is to make traditional Filipino dishes accessible to everyone. Whether you're craving classic dishes like adobo, lechon, or sinigang, we aim to deliver the finest meals right to your doorstep. Our team works with local Filipino chefs to ensure authenticity and quality in every dish.

Our Vision

We envision a world where anyone can experience the rich heritage of Filipino food. Our vision is to preserve the culinary traditions of the Philippines while embracing modern technology to provide a seamless, enjoyable experience for food lovers everywhere.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of passionate food lovers, dedicated to curating a menu that represents the true spirit of Filipino cuisine. We work hard to ensure that every dish is made fresh, with authentic ingredients, and with attention to detail. From our chefs to our delivery partners, each member of Pinoy Plates is committed to delivering a delightful culinary experience.
